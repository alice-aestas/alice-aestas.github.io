Csettrecsran

Aestas Hyacinth lay under the shadows of a large hawthorn tree, one arm over her eyes. The grass gently brushed her cheeks, blown by the soft summer breeze.

She was actually supposed to be in the citadel preparing for the upcoming summer festival. But she had snuck out instead, and ran all the way downhill to the south-west borough of Annyo city to join her friends at the crook in the woods.

The annual festival, a four-day event, was held to celebrate the start of the summer, and by coincidence, Aestas Hyacinth’s birthday was on the second day.

There would be a fair, and numerous parades in Annyo, and a large ball held in the citadel, where a celebration of Aestas’ birthday would take place. The long anticipated list of new recruits and newly appointed officials for the royal army would also be announced that day.
